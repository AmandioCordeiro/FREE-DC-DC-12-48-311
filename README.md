# FREE-DC-DC   (12 || 24 || 48) --> 321V
Step up DC DC  12 or 48 or 24 to 321v 

This is an Open-source hardware project of step-up ressonant converters.

LTSPICE models:
-   12 to 321 volts
-   24 to 321 volts
-   48 to 321 volts
